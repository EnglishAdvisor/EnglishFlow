Working with Words A — Responsibilities · Unit 4

To explain your job, you need 7 magic verbs. With them you can describe almost any responsibility at work:

workfor a company
livein a city
makeproducts
managea team / money
meetcustomers
havepeople in a team
sellproducts
Model — Listen to the teacher STUDY
Rafael (example): "My name's Rafael. I'm from Brazil and I live in Macaé. I work for an energy company. We make equipment for platforms. I'm a technician and I have five people in my team. We meet clients every week."
Activity 1 — Complete with the verbs BY WRITTEN

Use: work · live · make · manage · meet · have · sell

  1. I ________ for TechBR Solutions.
  2. I'm from Minas, but I ________ in Rio.
  3. We ________ software for banks.
  4. I ________ the Sales Department.
  5. I ________ four people in my team.
  6. They ________ customers every day.
  7. They ________ the products to companies in South America.
Activity 2 — About YOU BY WRITTEN + AUDIO

Write your own presentation (4–6 sentences) using at least 4 of the 7 verbs. Follow Rafael's model. Then 🎙️ record yourself reading it.
