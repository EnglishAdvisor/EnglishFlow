In Unit 1 you practise introductions and greetings in professional contexts. This capsule focuses on expressions for meeting colleagues for the first time — study the cards, order the conversation, and complete the sentences.
Key Expressions — First Meetings at Work
Introducing yourself
Hello! My name is [name].
Hi! I'm [name]. I'm new here.
I work in the [department].
Use I'm (informal) or My name is (more formal).
Asking about someone
What do you do?
What department are you in?
Are you new here?
Reply: I'm a [job]. I'm in [department].
Reacting politely
Nice to meet you.
Pleased to meet you.
Good to meet you.
Always reply: Nice to meet you too.
Welcoming someone
Welcome to the company!
Let me show you around.
Is there anything I can help with?
Reply: Thank you very much!
