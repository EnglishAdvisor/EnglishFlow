Business Communication — Starting a phone call · Unit 2

Phone calls in English follow a simple script. If you know the script, you never panic on the phone. Study the key expressions on the side panel first. 👉

Key Expressions — Phone calls
📞 Answering a call
Good morning, (company name).
(Maria) speaking.
🙋 Asking for someone
Can I speak to (name), please?
Is (name) there?
Is (name) in the office?
✅ Saying yes · ❌ Saying no
Yes, of course. One moment.
No, I'm sorry, she's out.
No, it isn't. It's (Clara).
👋 Ending a call
OK. Thanks. / Goodbye.
💡 On the phone, always say your company's name when you answer.
