Practically Speaking — Sequencing words · Unit 4
The sequencing words STUDY
first of all → then → next → after that → finally

We use them to describe the stages of a process, one after another.

Activity 1 — Listen and order the stages BY AUDIO + WRITTEN

Listen to the teacher describe how the robots at a warehouse do their job. Number the stages 1–5:

  1. ___ the person takes the correct items for the order
  2. ___ the warehouse computer receives customer orders
  3. ___ the robot returns the box and starts again
  4. ___ the robot finds the box and delivers it to a co-worker
  5. ___ the computer tells a robot to find the correct box

Activity 3 — Your process at work BY AUDIO

Think of a process from your typical day at work. 🎙️ Record yourself describing the stages with the sequencing words.

Example: First of all, I check emails. Then, I send new orders. Next, I …

✍️ Complete activities 1–2, then 🎙️ record your process (Activity 3).

Key Words
First of allThe 1st stage
ThenThe next stage
NextThe following stage
After thatThe stage after
FinallyThe last stage
