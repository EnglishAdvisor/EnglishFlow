💡 The golden rule: a company produces products (things you can touch) and provides services (things people do for you).

Activity 2 — The 6 business verbs BY WRITTEN

Complete this presentation of Sabor do Sul, a Brazilian food company, with: employ · sell · export · provide · buy · develop

Sabor do Sul is a Brazilian company and we produce 200 million litres of fruit juice every year. We 1______ around 2,000 people in total. We 2______ juice to Europe and Asia. We also 3______ new products for the health industry. Supermarkets and restaurants 4______ our products, and we 5______ them online too. We also 6______ lessons in healthy cooking!
