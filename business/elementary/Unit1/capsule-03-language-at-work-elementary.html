Grammar — Present Simple: be
Complete the table with the correct forms of be.
PositiveNegativeQuestionShort answer
I I am / I'm I am not / I'm not Am I ...? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
You/We/They You 're / You are You aren't Are you/we/they ...? Yes, you/we/they are.
No, you/we/they aren't.
He/She/It He 's / He is He isn't Is he/she/it ...? Yes, he/she/it is.
No, he/she/it isn't.
💡 Tip: Use 'm, 's or 're for speaking and informal writing. Use am, is, are for short answers: Are you at work? Yes, I am. NOT Yes, I'm.
