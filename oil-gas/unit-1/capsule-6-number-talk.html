Number Talk — 1 to 199
Two ways to say numbers:
📞 Telephone & reference numbers → say one digit at a time
   01238 = oh-one-two-three-eight
   4920 = four-nine-two-oh

📦 Quantities → say as a number
   130 = a hundred and thirty
   49 = forty-nine

⚠️ 13 (thirTEEN) vs 30 (THIRty) — stress on different syllable!
   15 (fifTEEN) vs 50 (FIFty) — 16 (sixTEEN) vs 60 (SIXty)
Activity 1 — Warm up your numbers

Count aloud from 1 to 25 — no writing, just say them.

Then count in tens: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90.

  • Your employee or ID number (digit by digit)
