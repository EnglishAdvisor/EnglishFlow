Project — Oil and gas employers in your country

Research the oil and gas industry in your country. Find out about the main companies and what types they are (NOC, operating, drilling, or service company).

Research these four areas
🏛️
Is there an NOC?
What is the name of the national oil company? Who owns it? How big is it?
🛢️
Operating companies
Which operating companies work in your country? Are they national or international?
⚙️
Drilling companies
Which drilling companies operate here? What type of wells do they drill?
🔧
Service companies
Which international service companies (SLB, Baker Hughes, etc.) have offices here?
Present your findings

Prepare a short presentation (2–3 minutes) using these sentence starters:

  • In [my country], the national oil company is called…
  • The main operating companies here are…
  • There are also several service companies, including…
  • The industry employs approximately… people.

✍️ Write your key findings and send via WhatsApp. Then record your presentation as an audio message.
