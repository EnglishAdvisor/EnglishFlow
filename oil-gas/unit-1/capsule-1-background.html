Oil and gas is an international industry. More than forty countries produce oil and gas. Brazil is one of the big producers. There are offshore platforms in the sea and onshore plants on land, all over the world.

The companies are international too. A national oil company (NOC) belongs to the government of a country. An operating company operates the wells and the platforms. A service company supplies equipment, tools, and special services to the other companies.

The people come from many countries. On one platform in Brazil, you can meet workers from Brazil, Norway, the USA, and Angola. They work together in teams: technicians, operators, and engineers.

At work, these teams speak English. It is the common language of the industry. Clear English is important for safety: a mistake with a number or with the name of a tool is dangerous on a platform. That is why you are here. Welcome to Unit 1.
