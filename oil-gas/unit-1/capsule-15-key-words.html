Key Words — Unit 1 · An International Industry
Adjectives
foreign adj.
from another country; not native
"Many foreign workers join international oil teams."
international adj.
involving two or more countries
"Oil and gas is an international industry."
offshore adj./adv.
in or on the sea, away from land
"She works on an offshore platform in the Campos Basin."
onshore adj./adv.
on land, not at sea
"He prefers onshore work — he lives near the oilfield."
Nouns
control room n.
room where operations are monitored and controlled
"The engineer monitors pressure from the control room."
drilling company n.
company that drills oil and gas wells
"The drilling company provides crews and equipment."
oilfield n.
area with underground oil reserves being extracted
"Ghawar is the world's largest oilfield, in Saudi Arabia."
oilwell n.
a hole drilled to extract oil from underground
"They drilled a new oilwell in the desert."
operating company n.
company that explores for and produces oil and gas
"BP is an operating company with global licences."
plant n.
a factory or industrial facility
"The processing plant separates oil, gas, and water."
service company n.
company providing technical services to operators
"SLB is a major service company operating worldwide."
team n.
group of people working together
"Our team has twenty technicians from five countries."
technician n.
person with specialist technical skills
"She is a maintenance technician on the platform."
Verbs
operate v.
to control or run a machine, system, or business
"They operate oil platforms in the Gulf of Mexico."
supply v.
to provide something that is needed
"Service companies supply equipment and expertise."
