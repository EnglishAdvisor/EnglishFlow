Sentence stress — the key rule
How sentence stress works in English
In English, we stress the most important words in a sentence — the words that carry the key meaning. Unstressed words (articles, prepositions, pronouns) are spoken more quickly and softly. Content words (nouns, main verbs, adjectives, adverbs) are usually stressed.
Example from the audio
We must put the geophones in the right place.
The underlined words carry the key information — they are stressed.
