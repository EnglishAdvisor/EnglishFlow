Some science — three types of professionals
Geologist
Studies rocks and rock layers underground
Physicist
Studies natural forces: heat, light, sound, electricity
Geophysicist
Uses physics to study geology — seismic surveys

Waves — types and properties
Types of waves
Sound waves
mechanical waves — travel through rock, water, and air
Light waves
electromagnetic — travel very fast, reflect off surfaces
Radio waves
electromagnetic — used in communication
Ocean waves
mechanical — move water, travel slowly
Seismic waves
sound waves in rock — used to find oil traps

Science vocabulary
geo- — prefix meaning "earth"
-logy — suffix meaning "the study of"
-phone — suffix meaning "sound"
hydro- — prefix meaning "water"
vibration — fast up-and-down movement
electrical signal — information as electricity
reflect — to send back (waves, light)
convert — to change one thing into another
