It's My Job
Saresh Budhrani
Rig Medic · Offshore Drilling Rig

My name is Saresh Budhrani and I am a rig medic on an offshore drilling rig. I work on board the rig, and my job is to keep the workers healthy and safe.

When someone is sick or injured, I take care of them. If someone is badly injured or very sick, I arrange evacuation — we use a helicopter or a support vessel to get them to a hospital on shore.

When new employees arrive on the rig, I give them basic health and safety training. I teach them what to do in an emergency, where the emergency equipment is, and how to use it.

We have a fire-fighting plan and a full evacuation plan for the rig. In case of a serious emergency, we need to get everyone off the rig quickly and safely. I help to coordinate these plans.

If there are problems with noise, waste management, or hygiene on the rig, I also deal with those. My job is to make sure the rig is a healthy environment for everyone.
