For each situation, answer: a) What will you do? and b) Which emergency equipment will you use?

1
Rigger — fallen from height
serious
A rigger has fallen from a high place. His leg is badly broken. He is conscious but in a lot of pain. He cannot move.
  • aWhat will you do?
  • bWhich equipment will you use?
Useful language: I'll check the area for danger. Then I'll try to help him. I'll use the ___. I'll call emergency services.
2
Technician — cut arm
minor
A technician has cut his arm on a sharp metal edge. It is bleeding but it is a minor injury. He is conscious and able to walk.
  • aWhat will you do?
  • bWhich equipment will you use?
3
Electrician — electric shock
serious
An electrician has received an electric shock. He is unconscious on the floor. The power is still on nearby.
  • aWhat will you do?
  • bWhich equipment will you use?
Remember: Never touch an unconscious person near live electricity without switching off the power first.
4
Welder — burned arm
minor
A welder has burned his arm on hot metal. The burn is painful but not life-threatening. He is conscious and standing.
  • aWhat will you do?
  • bWhich equipment will you use?
5
Workshop fire — manager with smoke inhalation
serious
There is a fire in the workshop. The workshop manager has just come out. He is conscious but having trouble breathing due to smoke.
  • aWhat will you do?
  • bWhich equipment will you use?
