Scenario A — Fire in the workshop
A small fire has started near a welding station. Smoke is spreading. Workers are nearby.
Scenario B — Fall from height
A rigger has fallen from a ladder and is lying on the ground. He is not moving.
Scenario C — Electric shock
An electrician has received an electric shock. He is unconscious on the floor.
Scenario D — Chemical leak
A pipe is leaking a toxic chemical. Workers in the area are coughing and feeling unwell.
Activity 1 — For each scenario, answer the questions
  • 1What is the main danger in this situation?
  • 2Who could be injured — and how?
  • 3What emergency equipment would you need?
  • 4Who would you call?
Activity 2 — Your opinion
  • 1Have you ever seen or been involved in an emergency at work? What happened?
  • 2What emergency training have you done? (first aid, fire drills, SCBA, etc.)
  • 3What do you think is the most important thing to do in an emergency?
Task: Answer in writing OR record a short audio. Send via WhatsApp.
