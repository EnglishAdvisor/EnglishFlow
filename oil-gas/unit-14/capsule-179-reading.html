When there's an accident…
Step 1 — Assess the situation
  • Try to understand what happened. Do this quickly and calmly.
  • Check for danger. If something injured somebody, will it also injure you?
  • Never do something that will injure you. If you do, there may be two injured people.
Step 2 — Make the area safe
  • Protect the injured person from danger.
  • Be careful — don't put yourself at risk.
Step 3 — Assess the injured people and give emergency first aid
  • Assess each person.
  • Help the people with the worst injuries first.
  • Only treat an injured person if you are competent to do so.
Step 4 — Get help
  • Call emergency services or make sure that someone has called them.
When there's a fire — REACT
R
Remove people from danger if you can do it safely.
E
Ensure the doors are closed to stop the smoke and fire spreading.
A
Activate the fire alarm.
C
Call emergency services.
T
Try to put out the fire or help people leave the area.
