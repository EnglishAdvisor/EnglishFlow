Pronunciation/v/ and /w/
🌐 This page is in English on purpose. If your browser offers to translate it, please decline / Por favor, não traduza esta página.
/v/
Upper teeth touch the lower lip. Voice vibrates.
Examples: vehicle, evacuate, activate
/w/
Lips rounded and pushed forward. No teeth on lip.
Examples: water, welding, worst

Activity 2 — Minimal pairs: can you hear the difference?

These pairs sound almost the same. Practise saying them. The difference is /v/ vs /w/.

vents/went
The gas vents on deck. — Where did he went? (→ go)
veal/wheel
Veal is a type of meat. The steering wheel.
viper/wiper
A viper is a snake. A wiper cleans the windscreen.
vine/wine
A grape vine. A glass of wine.
