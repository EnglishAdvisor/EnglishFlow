Listening — Radio Communication · Unit 8
📻 PTT Radio — How it works: Press To Talk (PTT) radios are used offshore for short-range communication. Only one person can speak at a time. When finished, say "Over" to hand the conversation back. Say "Out" when the conversation ends completely.
Control: Alpha platform, this is Control. Do you copy? Over.
Dave: Control, this is Alpha platform. Copy loud and clear. Over.
Control: Dave, pressure on well head area is increasing. Can you check the gauge? Over.
Dave: Affirmative. Stand by. [pause] Reading 85 kilopascals. Still within range. Over.
Control: Roger that. Continue to monitor. If it reaches 100, shut down the line. Over.
Dave: Understood. Will monitor and report. Is there anything else? Over.
Control: Negative. Good work, Dave. Control out.
Dave: Alpha platform out.
