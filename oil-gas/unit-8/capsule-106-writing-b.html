Writing B — Writing Bank — Formal Leave Request · Unit 8

A formal leave request is a written message asking your supervisor for time off. It must include: type of leave, dates, reason, and a polite closing.

Model Email — Annual Leave Request
To: J. Mitchell (Supervisor)  |  From: Rafael Souza  |  Subject: Annual Leave Request

Dear Mr Mitchell,

I am writing to request annual leave from 14th to 21st June. I would like to attend my sister's wedding, which takes place on 16th June in Niterói.

My duties will be covered by Tom Allen during my absence. I have already informed Tom about all ongoing tasks and briefed him on the current instrument readings.

I would be grateful if you could approve this request at your earliest convenience. Please let me know if you require any further information.

Kind regards,
Rafael Souza
Instrument Technician — Esperança-1
Activities — Choose one scenario and write a similar email (60–90 words)
Scenario A
Your sister is getting married next month. You need 5 days off. Your colleague will cover your duties.
Scenario B
You injured your hand at home and cannot work for 3 days. You need sick leave. Attach a doctor's note.
Scenario C
Your son is ill and in hospital. You need emergency leave for 2 days. Your supervisor needs to know urgently.

Include in your email: greeting · reason for leave · dates · who covers your duties · polite closing
