Activity 1 — Role-play: Pressure check
Student A (Control): Call the platform and ask Dave to check the temperature on the separator. Tell him to shut down if it goes above 80°C.
Student B (Dave): Respond, check the reading (72°C) and report back to Control.
Activity 2 — Role-play: Emergency call
Student A (Dave): Call Control to report a small gas leak near the well head area. Ask for instructions.
Student B (Control): Tell Dave to evacuate the area, activate the alarm, and stand by for the safety team.

🎙️ Practise both role-plays. Focus on using correct phrases, clear pronunciation, and natural rhythm. Then record one complete role-play and send the audio via WhatsApp.
