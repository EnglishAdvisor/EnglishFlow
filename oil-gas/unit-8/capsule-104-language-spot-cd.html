More practice — Comparatives round 2 💪

Same grammar, new challenges. You know the rule — now make it automatic.

Exercise 1 — Write the comparative form
  1. safe → ______    (This platform is ______ than the old one.)
  2. expensive → ______    (Helicopter transport is ______ than the supply boat.)
  3. busy → ______    (The day shift is ______ than the night shift.)
  4. good → ______    (The food this hitch is ______ than last time.)
  5. bad → ______    (The weather today is ______ than yesterday.)
Exercise 2 — Rewrite with (not) as … as

Example: The night shift is quieter than the day shift. → The day shift is not as quiet as the night shift.

  1. The FPSO is bigger than the drilling rig. → The drilling rig ____________.
  2. Safety and production are equally important. → Safety is ____________ production.
  3. My cabin is smaller than yours. → My cabin ____________ yours.
Exercise 3 — Find and fix the mistake
  1. The new crane is more bigger than the old one. → ______
  2. This job is importanter than people think. → ______
  3. The sea today is as calm than yesterday. → ______
  4. My English is gooder than last month! → ______

Grammar at a glance — Comparatives
A
B
A is bigger than B A > B
The derrick is taller than the crane.
A
B
A is as big as B A ≈ B
Safety is as important as production.
A
B
A is not as big as B A < B
A drilling rig is not as big as a production platform.
Irregular: good → better · bad → worse · far → farther
⚠️ Never say more bigger — use -er OR more, never both.
