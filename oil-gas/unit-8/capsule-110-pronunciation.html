Pronunciation — Consonant Clusters · Unit 8
What is a consonant cluster? Two or more consonant sounds together with no vowel between them. Portuguese speakers often add a vowel sound (epenthesis) — English keeps them tight. Example: stress → not "es-tress", but "stress" /stɹɛs/.
