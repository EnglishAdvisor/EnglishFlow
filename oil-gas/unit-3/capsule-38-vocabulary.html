1Describing positions

Use these words to describe where things are on a control panel or computer desk:

QuestionPosition wordReference
What's the thing...above / belowthe keyboard
What are the things...next to / in / onthe screen
on the left / right
Example: A: What's the thing above the keyboard? B: That's a screen.
2Problem sentences — Present Continuous

Make sentences about computer problems. Use the verb in brackets.

The screen (show) an error message. → The screen is showing an error message.
The gauge (not work) properly.
The computer (make) a strange noise.
Some of the keys (not work).
The red light (flash).
