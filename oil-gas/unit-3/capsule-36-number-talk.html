1Industry problems — solve and say aloud
Problem 1
Li is working three twelve-hour night shifts this week. How many hours is she working this week?
Say: "Three times twelve equals..."
Problem 2
A petrol tanker has 18,500 litres of petrol in it now. It can carry 30,000 litres on the road. How much more petrol can the driver load into the tanker?
Say: "Thirty thousand minus eighteen thousand five hundred equals..."
Problem 3
How many 8,000-litre tanks do you need for 32,000 litres of oil?
Say: "Thirty-two thousand divided by eight thousand equals..."
Problem 4
A refinery produces 6.2 million litres of petrol per day and 0.75 million litres of other products. What is the total daily production?
Say: "Six point two plus zero point seven five equals..."
