Task — Explain a refinery process
Choose one process below. Study the steps, then record a short audio explaining the process in your own words. Use sequence words and passive verbs where possible.
Process A — Loading a bulk tanker at the refinery
  • Drive the tanker into the loading area.
  • Earth the tanker (connect to ground).
  • Switch off the master switch.
  • Check the transport emergency card.
  • Connect the pipes.
  • Load the tanker with the petroleum product.
  • Drain the hoses.
  • Drive the tanker out of the loading area.
Process B — How fractional distillation works
  • Crude oil is pumped into the furnace and heated.
  • The boiling oil enters the bottom of the distillation tower.
  • The oil is separated into fractions (parts) by boiling point.
  • The lightest products (petroleum gas) rise to the top.
  • The heaviest products (asphalt) sink to the bottom.
  • Each fraction is piped out of the tower at its boiling point level.
  • Products are stored in tanks in the refinery.
  • Products are transported by tanker, rail, or pipeline.
