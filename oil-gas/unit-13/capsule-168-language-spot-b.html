Activity 1 — How is a bulk tanker loaded safely? (8 steps)

Read the actions. Then reveal the passive sentence for each step. Use sequence words: First · Then · Next · After that · Finally

1 First
Drive the tanker into the loading area.
First, the tanker is driven into the loading area.
2 Then
Earth the tanker. (connect to ground — safety)
Then, the tanker is earthed.
3 Next
Switch off the master switch.
Next, the master switch is switched off.
4 After that
Check the transport emergency card.
After that, the transport emergency card is checked.
5 Then
Connect the pipes.
Then, the pipes are connected.
6 Next
Load the tanker.
Next, the tanker is loaded.
7 After that
Drain the hoses.
After that, the hoses are drained.
8 Finally
Drive the tanker out of the loading area.
Finally, the tanker is driven out of the loading area.
Activity 2 — Key vocabulary for loading
earth (v)connect electrical device to ground (BrE; AmE = ground)
transport emergency cardinfo card about product + emergency procedure for the driver
master switchmain power switch — must be off before loading
hoseflexible pipe used to transfer liquids
drain (v)remove all liquid from the hoses after loading
bulk tankerlarge tank truck that carries liquid products
Sequence Words
First, ...step 1
Then, ...next step
Next, ...following step
After that, ...after the previous step
Finally, ...last step
Passive Pattern
drive → is drivenirregular
earth → is earthedregular
load → is loadedregular
drain → are drainedregular
