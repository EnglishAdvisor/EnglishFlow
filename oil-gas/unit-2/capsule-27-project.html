Project — Oil and gas fields in your country
Research task: Find information about oil and gas fields (or upstream companies) in your country. Use the internet or your own knowledge from work. You will report back on what you found.
Activity 1 — Research questions
1.What are the main oil or gas fields in your country? Give at least one name.
2.Where are they located? (region / offshore / onshore?)
3.What companies operate there? Are they national or international?
4.Do you know how much oil or gas they produce? (bbl/d or m³/d)
5.What step is the field at? (exploration / drilling / development / production)
Activity 2 — Example report

Example: "In Saudi Arabia, the Ghawar field is in the Eastern Province. It is onshore. Saudi Aramco operates it. It produces about 3.8 million barrels per day. It is in the production stage."

Now write your own report using your country's information.

📝 Answer in writing OR record a short audio. Send via WhatsApp.
