It's My Job — Well Test Operator
Steve Dumont
Well Test Operator
📍 Canada  |  ⏱ 12-hour shifts

"My name is Steve Dumont and I am a well test operator. I work for a service company in Canada. My job is to test new oil and gas wells after drilling. I prepare the test equipment and connect it to the well. Then I start the test. I record the data — the pressure and the temperature — and I send the data to the engineers. The engineers study the data and decide: Is this a good well? My shift is 12 hours. I work outside in all weather conditions — sometimes very cold!"

Activity 1 — Comprehension questions
1. What type of company does Steve work for?
2. When does Steve test a well — before or after drilling?
3. What two things does Steve record during a test?
4. Who studies the data? What do they decide?
Activity 2 — Your job
Compare your job (or a job you know) with Steve's job. What is similar? What is different? Describe your own work briefly.
