Kick Off — Upstream & Downstream

Two sides of the same barrel

On a Campos Basin platform, nobody says "the oil business" — they say upstream or downstream, depending on which side of the well they work on.

The upstream sector is the crew that locates and produces crude oil — from the first seismic survey to the platform that lifts it out of the seabed.

The downstream sector takes over once the crude leaves the field: refining it into fuel, diesel, and everything that ends up in a tank or a pipeline to a customer.
