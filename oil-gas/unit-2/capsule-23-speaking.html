Activity 1 — Use Wh- questions to ask about their jobs

Use do/does + Wh- words to ask questions:

What does Igor do?

He's a driller. He supervises the drilling crew.

Where does Andrea work?

She works in West Africa and in the office.

1. What _______ Igor do? (job)
2. Where _______ he work?
3. How many hours _______ he work per day?
4. What _______ Andrea study?
5. Why _______ she like her job?
