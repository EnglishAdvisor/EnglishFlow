Modal verbs — can and must
CAN / CAN'T
can = ability / possibility
can't = not possible

"I can lift 50 kg."
"The crane can hold 5 tonnes."
"I can't see the load."
MUST / MUSTN'T
must = obligation / rule
mustn't = prohibition

"You must wear a hard hat."
"You mustn't smoke here."
"Workers must sign in."
SubjectModalVerb (base)Example
I / He / ShecanliftHe can lift 70 kilos.
I / He / Shecan'tliftShe can't lift 70 kilos.
You / WorkersmustwearYou must wear a hard hat.
You / Workersmustn'tsmokeWorkers mustn't smoke here.
