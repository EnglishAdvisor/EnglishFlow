Writing Bank 4 — A safety incident report
Reporting a safety incident
In the oil and gas industry, workers must report all accidents and near misses in writing. A safety incident report tells: what happened, where and when, who was involved, and what action was taken.
1
Date and location
Write the date, time, and exact location.
Date: 10 June 2026 · Time: 09:30 · Location: Deck B, Platform 4
2
What happened
Describe the incident clearly using Past Simple.
A worker slipped on a wet surface near the crane.
3
The hazard
Identify the hazard and say what caused it.
The surface was wet because of a chemical spill.
4
Action taken
Say what was done immediately after the incident.
The area was closed. The worker received first aid.
5
Recommendation
Use must / should to say what should change.
Workers must wear non-slip boots. The area should have a warning sign.
Model incident report
From: Ali Hassan, Safety Officer · To: Supervisor's Report File
Date: 8 June 2026 · Time: 14:15 · Location: Pump Room, Level 2

What happened: A rigger slipped on the floor near Pump 3 and fell. He hurt his left arm.

The hazard: The floor was wet because of an oil leak from a pipe joint. There was no warning sign in the area.

Action taken: The rigger received first aid immediately. The area was closed and cleaned. Maintenance checked the pipe.

Recommendation: All workers must check floors before starting work. Warning signs must be placed in wet areas. The pipe joint should be replaced.
