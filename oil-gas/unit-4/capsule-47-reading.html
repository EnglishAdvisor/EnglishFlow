Safety signs — colours and shapes
type 1⚠️WarningBlack & yellow triangle
type 2🚫ProhibitionRed & white circle
type 3🔵MandatoryBlue & white circle
type 4🟢InformationGreen & white square
⚠️ Type 1 — Warning signs (black and yellow triangles)
These signs warn us about hazards. Examples: "Danger! Overhead crane" or "High voltage". Be careful near these signs.
🚫 Type 2 — Prohibition signs (red and white circles)
These signs tell us we must NOT do something. Examples: "Do not smoke here" or "Do not switch off this machine".
🔵 Type 3 — Mandatory signs (blue and white circles)
These tell you what you MUST wear or do. Examples: "Wear goggles" or "Read the instructions before you use the machine".
🟢 Type 4 — Information signs (green and white squares)
These give information about safety. Examples: "This way to the emergency exit" or "Lifejackets are here".
