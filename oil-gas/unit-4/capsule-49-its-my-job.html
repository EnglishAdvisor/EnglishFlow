It's my job — Danny King, Rigger
👷
Danny King
Rigger · Offshore Platform
Teacher audio — teacher-audio-unit4-its-my-job.mp3
Listen and check your answers.

Danny King is a rigger on an offshore platform. Riggers prepare lifting equipment. They work with cranes to lift heavy loads. First, they attach slings to a load. Then the crane lifts the load.

Danny often works at high places on the platform. He always wears a safety harness because a fall from height can kill you. He must decide the weight and size of every load before he lifts it. He estimates the weight carefully because a crane can only lift a maximum load. Every day he checks the equipment.

The most important thing in his job is safety. "The load is in the sling. You must know where it's going, and you must know its weight and size. Every day, every lift. No shortcuts."

Danny likes his job because he works outside in the fresh air, he likes the challenge of high places, and he works with a good team.

Key vocabulary
rigger — prepares lifting equipment
crane — large machine for lifting
sling — rope/chain that holds a load
load — what is being lifted
safety harness — prevents falls
estimate — calculate approximately
attach — connect, fit together
hazard — a possible danger
