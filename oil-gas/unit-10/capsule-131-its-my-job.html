Ahmed works for Kuwait Oil Company. His job title is Safety and Environment Officer.

Ahmed's main responsibility is to work with contractors — companies that do jobs for Kuwait Oil Company. He helps them understand how to work safely and respect the environment.

Before contractors start a job, Ahmed does risk assessments with them. A risk assessment identifies possible dangers and explains how to avoid accidents.

Last week, Ahmed worked on a construction project. He visited construction sites and talked to workers. He explained why health, safety, and the environment (HSE) is important for every person on site.

Key Vocabulary
contractor — a company or person employed to do specific work for another organisation
risk assessment — a written report identifying possible hazards and how to control them
construction site — a location where building or installation work is being carried out
HSE — Health, Safety, and Environment — a department or set of standards
safely — without causing danger or harm
