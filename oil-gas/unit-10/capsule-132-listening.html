Grammar Reminder
It happened — Past Simple (not "it's happened")
Did it work? — Question with base form
It worked — Past Simple positive
It started — Past Simple (use for specific past time)
I waited — Regular verb: wait → waited
Problem Vocabulary
oil spill — oil released accidentally into the environment
noisy compressor — a compressor making excessive sound
leak — uncontrolled escape of fluid or gas from a pipe or fitting
