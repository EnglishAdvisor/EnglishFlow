Understanding the Scene
🟫
Contained
The spill is inside a bund / berm — a wall that prevents oil from spreading further. The damage is limited.
🌊
Uncontained
No bund or berm — oil is spreading freely. Emergency response required immediately.

BrE: bund · AmE: berm — a low wall built around a tank to contain spills

Useful Language
There's been an incident at...
There's an oil spill between...
It happened at / around...
About [X] litres spilled.
There are no injuries / There are [X] injuries.
The spill is contained / uncontained.
We've closed the valve.
The area is safe / not safe.
Reporting Questions
Where is it?
When did it happen?
How much oil spilled?
Are there any injuries?
Is it contained?
What action did you take?
