Key Vocabulary
fire — uncontrolled burning; use water or foam to extinguish
explosion — sudden violent burst of energy
oil spill — release of oil into the environment (land or sea)
gas leak — uncontrolled release of gas from a pipe or well
wildlife protection programme — plan to study and protect animals and plants in a project area
safety procedures — standard steps workers follow to prevent accidents
special drilling techniques — e.g., horizontal/extended reach drilling to avoid sensitive areas
