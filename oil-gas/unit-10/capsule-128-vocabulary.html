eco-hazard
something that can harm the environment: people, plants, animals, water, earth, or air
incident
something that happens, an event: a fire, a gas leak, an oil spill, a collision, an accident

Eco-hazard Types
fire — uncontrolled burning
explosion — sudden, violent burst
oil spill — oil released into the environment
gas leak — uncontrolled release of gas
collision — crash between two objects
Prevention Methods
wildlife protection programme — plan to keep animals safe
safety procedures — standard steps to prevent accidents
special drilling techniques — methods to protect the environment during drilling
