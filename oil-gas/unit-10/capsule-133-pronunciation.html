-ed Sounds
/id/ — extra syllable
After verbs ending in /t/ or /d/
start→started · wait→waited
/t/ — voiceless
After voiceless consonants (k, p, f, s, sh, ch)
work→worked · finish→finished
/d/ — voiced
After vowels and most voiced consonants
happen→happened · close→closed
