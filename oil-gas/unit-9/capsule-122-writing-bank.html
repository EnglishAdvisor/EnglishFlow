Writing Tips
• Use noun phrases (not full sentences) for tasks
• Work completed = jobs fully finished this shift
• Work not completed = jobs started or not started
• Include job card numbers (e.g. E4096)
• If ongoing: add "Materials collected" or "Installation ongoing"
• Notes section: anything unusual (absence, delays)
