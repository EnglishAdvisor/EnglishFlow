Past Simple — be (was / were)

Positive

Consumption was 1 trillion m³ in 1970.
They were in China last month.
I / He / She / It → was
You / We / They → were

Negative

We weren't busy yesterday.
I wasn't at work last week.
I / He / She / It → wasn't
You / We / They → weren't

Question

Was the ship late?
Were you busy last week?
I / He / She / It → Was ___?
You / We / They → Were ___?

Short answers

Yes, it was. / No, it wasn't.
Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.
Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.
