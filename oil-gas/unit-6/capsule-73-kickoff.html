✓ The drill string (B).

✓ The mud pump (E).

2Complete the description of the mud process
Words: mud pump · mud tank · drill string · bit · rock cuttings · mud screen · mud tank
Drilling mud is a mixture of water, clay, and other materials. The mud pump pumps mud from the mud tank into the top of the drill string. The mud flows down inside the drill string and cools the bit. It cleans the bit and carries rock cuttings up with it. The mud and cuttings go to the mud screen. The mud screen separates the cuttings from the mud. The mud flows through to the mud tank below.
