Problem 1
Problem:
The cuttings aren't coming up to the surface.
Why?
The mud is too thin.
Solution:
Make it thicker.
How?
Add 250 kilos of clay.
Problem 2
Problem:
The mud pump is very noisy.
Why?
The mud is too thick.
Solution:
Make it thinner.
How?
Add 2,000 litres of water.
Problem 3
Problem:
The drilling floor isn't safe / clean.
Why?
There's a lot of mud on the floor.
Solution:
Clean the floor.
How?
With water.

Mud problems
too thin — not viscous enough; cuttings don't rise
too thick — too viscous; pump strains and gets noisy
thicker — add clay to make mud denser
thinner — add water to reduce viscosity
clay — heavy earth added to thicken mud
viscous — thick and sticky (like honey)
cuttings — rock pieces that mud must carry up
Adjective patterns
too thin = not thick enough
too thick = not thin enough
Make it thicker
Make it more viscous
