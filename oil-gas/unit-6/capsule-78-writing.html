Spelling — single or double letters
Rule 1 — Words ending in a single vowel + consonant
Double the consonant before -er, -est, -ing, -ed:
big → bigger, biggest | hot → hotter, hottest
drill → driller, drilling (double l already present)
Rule 2 — Words ending in -e
Drop the -e before -er, -ing:
drive → driver, driving | operate → operator, operating
