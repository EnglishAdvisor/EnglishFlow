Key Facts — Precision
•1 mm = 1,000 μm (one millimetre = one thousand microns)
•1 μm = 0.001 mm (one micron = point oh oh one millimetres)
•A CNC milling machine has a tolerance of ±25 μm
•The width of a human hair ≈ 100 μm
•The diameter of a pinhead ≈ 1 mm
•The length of a normal mosquito ≈ 10 mm
