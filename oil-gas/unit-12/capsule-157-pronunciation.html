The sounds /b/ and /p/
/b/
voiced bilabial stop
belt, bolt, broken, bent
/p/
voiceless bilabial stop
pump, pipe, pressure, problem
Both sounds use your two lips. The difference: /b/ uses your voice (feel your throat vibrate). /p/ is silent — air only. Say b-b-b then p-p-p and feel the difference.
