will
We use will when we decide what to do at the moment of speaking.
Immediate decision
AIt's leaking.
BI'll check it.
AThe belt is broken.
BI'll replace it.
We also use will to talk about future plans and predictions.
Future
AWhen will you finish?
BWe won't finish before midnight.
AWill Khalid be here tomorrow?
BNo, he won't. He will be here on Tuesday.

Structure
Positive: I/you/he/she/we/they will + verb
Short form: 'll → I'll, you'll, he'll
Negative: will not = won't + verb
Question: Will + subject + verb?
Short answers: Yes, I will. / No, I won't.
