Carlos Sanchez
Electrician — Oil & Gas Industry
Maintenance crew · Offshore platform
What Carlos does
  • Installs electrical wiring, fixtures, and control equipment
  • Carries out routine maintenance on all electrical systems
  • Troubleshoots and repairs electrical faults
  • Reads technical drawings and wiring diagrams
  • Follows all electrical safety procedures and permits to work
Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
⛑ Hard hat
🥽 Safety glasses (side shields)
👟 Safety shoes
🧤 Safety gloves
👂 Hearing protection
🧥 Fire retardant clothing
🪢 Safety harness
😷 Breathing apparatus
🟦 Insulating rubber matting
Hazards
⚡ Electrocution
Working with live electrical equipment can cause serious injury or death. Always isolate the circuit and apply a permit to work before starting any electrical repair.
🔥 Sparks near flammable gas
Electrical sparks are especially dangerous on offshore platforms where flammable gas may be present. Even a small spark can ignite a gas cloud and cause an explosion.
Activity — Answer the questions
1. Name three things Carlos installs or maintains.
Wiring, fixtures, and control equipment (accept any three from the list).
2. What two main hazards does Carlos face?
Electrocution and sparks near flammable gas.
3. Why is the insulating rubber matting important?
It insulates the electrician from the ground, reducing the risk of electric shock passing through the body.
