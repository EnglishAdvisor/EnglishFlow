In oil and gas facilities, equipment must be kept in good working condition at all times. A breakdown can stop production, create safety hazards, and cost money. For this reason, maintenance is one of the most critical functions in the industry.

There are two main types of maintenance. Routine maintenance — also called preventive maintenance — is carried out on a regular schedule to prevent breakdowns before they happen. Tasks are planned in advance and recorded in a maintenance schedule. For example, a generator may need an oil check every 8 hours, a battery inspection every month, and a spark plug replacement every 300 hours.

Corrective maintenance happens after a problem or breakdown is discovered. A technician troubleshoots the problem — identifying the cause — and then carries out the repair. The repair is recorded in a repair record.

Two key workers in maintenance teams are mechanics and electricians. Mechanics work on engines, pumps, generators, compressors, and other mechanical equipment. Electricians work on wiring, fixtures, motors, and control systems. Both carry out troubleshooting, repairs, and routine maintenance.

Communication is essential. Workers use job cards and repair records to track what has been done and what still needs to be done. A maintenance plan helps the team manager schedule the day's work and make sure all equipment stays safe and operational.

Key Terms
maintenancekeeping equipment in good working condition through regular checks and repairs
routine maintenanceplanned, scheduled tasks done regularly to prevent breakdowns
corrective maintenanceunplanned repair work done after a problem or failure occurs
troubleshootto investigate and identify the cause of a problem before repairing it
repair recorda document recording the problem found, its cause, and the repair made
mechanica technician who repairs engines, pumps, compressors, and mechanical parts
electriciana technician who installs and repairs wiring, motors, and electrical systems
breakdowna sudden failure of a machine or system that stops it from working
