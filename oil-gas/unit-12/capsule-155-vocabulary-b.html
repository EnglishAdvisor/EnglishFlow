Parts Glossary
belt — a loop of material that transfers power between two rotating parts
bolt — a threaded metal fastener that holds parts together
gear — a toothed wheel that transmits force and changes speed
cap — a small cover that seals an opening or pipe end
wire — a thin metal conductor that carries electrical current
angle grinder — a handheld power tool used to cut, grind, or remove rust
