Activity 1 — Problem words. Study the 4 adjectives.
frozen
cannot move — seized up, completely stuck
broken
cracked or in pieces — no longer intact
split
has a tear or crack along its length
bent
no longer straight — deformed by force

Activity 3 — Match problem to solution
The wires are corroded → I'll clean them. Match each problem with the correct solution.
1
The wires are corroded.
I'll clean them.
2
The tank is damaged. There's a big dent.

3

I'll re-start the system.
4
The copier is jammed.
I'll get the manual.
5
The bolt is rusted. I can't remove it.
I'll get the angle grinder.
6
The gear is worn.
I'll replace it.
7
The belt is loose. It's noisy.
I'll tighten it.
8
The cap is leaking.
I'll check it.
Problem Adjectives
corroded — damaged by rust or chemical reaction
damaged — physically harmed; has dents, cracks, or deformation
jammed — stuck and unable to move; blocked
rusted — covered with rust (iron + moisture)
worn — gradually damaged by use; needs replacing
loose — not tight or secure; can cause noise or failure
leaking — allowing fluid or gas to escape through a gap
